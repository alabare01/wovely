Bev does the fiddly part

Three steps between "found a pattern" and "hook in hand."

Step 1
Hand her anything
A bought PDF, photos of a paper pattern, a Ravelry link, or a blog URL. Bev reads them all — the original goes safely into your Vault.
Step 2
She checks the math
BevCheck verifies every row's stitch counts before you start. Errors get flagged on the exact row — no more discovering them at round 40.
Step 3
You just crochet
Tick rows as you go — your place kept on every device. Running low? Bev already counted your yardage and can order the difference.
