Direction
Playful Wovely lavender (option 2b): warm, tactile and fun without losing polish. Type is Fredoka for display and Nunito for everything else. Core palette: #7B6AD4 accent #FBF9FF canvas #2E2748 ink #FFC24B gold #5EC9AE mint. Gold is reserved for two things only: the yarn cord and premium (Craft) treatments — that scarcity is what makes premium feel special.

Signature elements
Bev, everywhere it matters
Mascot and guide: greets on import, narrates progress, presents premium personally, certifies BevCheck. Her voice = product voice.
The gold yarn cord
Real photographed yarn running the nav's right seam (bottom seam on mobile). Frayed, fuzzy, mirror-tiled, drop-shadowed. Our single strongest brand mark.
The Stitch icon set →

Coverfill image standard
Every cover: blurred scaled copy fills the frame, sharp full image centered on top. Any aspect ratio, no crops, no bars.
Woven texture

Blurred-fill + pin ribbon
Imported assets ride a horizontal ribbon; pins tilt gold and sort forward. Sections show only their own assets.

Free vs. Craft
Free$0 forever
  • 5 regular imports, full row tracking
  • Browse community patterns
  • One Advanced import as "Bev's treat" — the conversion taste
Craft$6.99/mo · $59.99 yearly
  • Unlimited storage + Advanced imports
  • Collections (MCAL/MKAL) + clue calendar
  • Vault, calculators, 24/7 human support
Premium feel: gold-yarn accents matching the cord, Craft badge + gold-edged cards on gated features, Bev presenting premium personally, and a vault-door unlock celebration on upgrade (queued). Paywall fires from the limit banner, locked add-card, and Craft import cards — never as an ambush.
5
Import & asset handling
Sources: PDF upload · photos of paper patterns · Ravelry link · paste a URL · type/paste text. Advanced adds multi-file reads (PDF + charts + schematics as one), stitch-chart parsing, and Bev's deeper pass for long/complex designs.
What happens to every import
  • Original file + all extracted images → the Vault
  • Cover auto-picked from the pattern's main image; swappable at review
  • Materials stored structured (color swatches, hook size — shoppable later)
  • Key assets (charts, schematics, step photos) tagged to their sections, served on the ribbon, pinnable
  • BevCheck validates rows & stitch counts → accuracy gauge, certified stamp
6
Collections — the MCAL/MKAL model
A Collection is a shell pattern with a clue calendar. Each clue drop is a mini-import Bev appends as a new Part; reminders fire on drop days; a group progress line ("clue 3 of 8 — 214 makers on pace") gives community energy without full social infrastructure. Bev opens each clue like an envelope — the recurring charm moment. Also covers plain bundles: related patterns grouped and tracked as one.
7
Charm & retention
BevCheck gauge
Needle dial swings with overshoot to the accuracy score, then a "Certified" stamp slaps down. An outcome, not a progress bar.
Bev talks during import
Rotating speech bubbles while she works — "Ooh, amigurumi!", "Counting your rows…". Voice, not a spinner.
Stitches earned
Profile achievements: First Stitch, Finisher, Five in a Row, Century Club, Frog Free, Deep Stash. Streaks feed retention.
Row counter
Big tactile −/+ with "Undo" / "Round done" captions. Bev keeps your place across devices.
