How it collapses
  • < 1100px: the Noticeboard rail moves from the right column to directly under the feed header, full width; feed stays a single centered column (max 680px).
  • < 768px: one column edge-to-edge. The sidebar becomes the fixed bottom tab bar (Yarn Circle = heart icon), exactly as the app shell already does in the 2b Responsive Spec.
  • The Noticeboard condenses to a single tappable "Noticeboard · N updates" strip pinned above the compose bar; tapping expands it in place.
  • Post cards keep every affordance (Lovely, comment, Hook this too, pattern chip); photo height drops to ~200px and buttons shrink to icon+count.
  • The compose bar's "Share a Lovely" button goes full-width; the weekly-theme banner stacks its button below the copy.
  • Comment composer stays a single-row input pinned to the bottom of an expanded post.
